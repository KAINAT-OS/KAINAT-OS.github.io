🌟 What is KainatOS?

KainatOS is a groundbreaking Linux distribution that combines the power and flexibility of Linux with a beautifully designed, intuitive user interface. We've taken the complexity out of the terminal and put you in the driver's seat with a seamless, graphical experience that's easy to use - yet powerful enough for advanced users.

Nvidia driver

Install the NVIDIA driver from Discover (KainatOS’s app center). Search for nvidia-driver-kainatos and you will find it. It should automatically detect if your PC has an NVIDIA GPU and then prompt you to install. If it doesn’t, you can always install it from the Discover app store or click here from KainatOS.
